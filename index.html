Crystal Collectors!

Each game you will be given a random number called "Goal."


The four crystals found below each have a random number assigned to them every game. Click them to add them to your Player Score.


If you match your Player Score to the Goal, you win! But if you exceed the Goal, you will lose that round.


After you either win or lose, a new Goal number will be generated, and the crystals will be assigned new hidden values.


Now go collect those crystals -- Good luck!
